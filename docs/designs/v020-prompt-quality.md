## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version is a taste engine that learns your naming style across sessions. Persistent preferences in ~/.namejam/taste.json. After 3-5 sessions, names feel like "yours." Plus cultural safety scoring and a name provenance engine. Not for this release — the foundation (quality generation) must be solid first.

### Approach Selected
Approach B: Manual evaluation + prompt rewrite. Run /namejam 5-10 times on real projects, personally rate every name, identify dud patterns, rewrite SKILL.md generation section with few-shot examples from real YC companies and beloved OSS repos.

The manual eval is a pre-coding task performed by the author. Its output (rated names, identified dud patterns) feeds directly into the SKILL.md rewrite. No separate evaluation artifact is produced.
